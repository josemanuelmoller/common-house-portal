How our OS works

Six agents running Common House's operating system.

Each agent holds a specific role — like a team member — and connects to the databases it needs to read or update. Hover any agent to see exactly what it touches.

The system runs autonomously, surfaces signals for human review, and never overwrites validated data without a gate.
